Game Instructions

Overview

Afterlife Metro is a 2D pixel-art narrative co-op adventure where players explore the boundary between life and death. You play as a human or ghost, cooperating to complete challenges and uncover the story.

Controls

  • Ghost: Arrow keys to move
  • Player: WASD to move

Gameplay Tips

  • Focus on your side of the screen to complete objectives.
  • There are some properties that only one player can interact and other can't.
  • Collect items and reach the endpoint as soon as possible.
  • Watch the timer to compete for best scores and get ranked at the leaderboard.

Starting the Game

Click the "Start Game" button on the homepage to load the game. Make sure your browser allows pop-ups if the iframe does not display properly.
